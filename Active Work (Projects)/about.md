- **What goes here:** Things with a strict deadline or an end state.
    
- **Examples:**
    
    - `Infosys Exam Prep 2026`
        
    - `Resume Finalization`
        
    - `Smart Water Pump Prototype`
        
    - `Relocation Logistics`
        
- **Why it works:** When a project is done (like when you finish your relocation or pass the exam), you move the entire folder out of here and into the Archive. This keeps your active workspace incredibly clean.
