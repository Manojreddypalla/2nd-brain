# Digital Content Creation (DCC) & Creative Technology

> The field focused on creating digital experiences, media, interactive entertainment, films, simulations, and visual content using art, engineering, and computer science.

---

# Purpose

Digital Content Creation (DCC) combines **art**, **technology**, and **engineering** to create:

- 🎮 Games
- 🎬 Movies
- 📺 TV Shows
- 🎨 Animation
- 💥 Visual Effects (VFX)
- 🖥️ CGI
- 📱 AR/VR Experiences
- 🏗️ Architectural Visualization
- 🚗 Automotive Visualization
- 🛰️ Simulations
- 📢 Motion Graphics
