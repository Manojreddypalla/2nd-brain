
> Predicts future outcomes by extracting insights from data using statistics, programming, and machine learning.

## Core Purpose

A Data Scientist answers questions like:

- Why are customers leaving?
    
- What products should we recommend?
    
- Will this transaction be fraudulent?
    
- How much inventory should we keep next month?
    

The goal is **decision-making using data**.

# Difference from ML Engineer

# Market Snapshot (India)

- **Primary value:** Turn raw data into actionable business insights and predictions.
    
- **Demand:** Strong across finance, e-commerce, healthcare, logistics, and technology companies.
    
- **Typical entry salary:** Around **₹6 LPA** (varies by company and skills).
    
- **Senior compensation:** Often **₹35–55+ LPA** in top companies.
    
- **Trend:** Demand remains high, but employers increasingly expect strong software engineering and AI skills in addition to statistics.
