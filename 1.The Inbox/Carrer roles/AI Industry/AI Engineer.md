# AI Engineer

> **An AI Engineer builds software products that use Artificial Intelligence to solve real-world problems.**

If a Machine Learning Engineer focuses on **models**, an AI Engineer focuses on the **entire AI application**.

---

# Purpose

An AI Engineer asks:

> "How do I use AI to build a useful product?"

Examples:

- AI chatbot
- AI coding assistant
- AI voice assistant
- AI search engine
- AI tutor
- AI medical assistant

# Difference from Machine Learning Engineer

|Machine Learning Engineer|AI Engineer|
|---|---|
|Builds and deploys ML models|Builds complete AI applications|
|Focuses on model performance|Focuses on user-facing products|
|Training pipelines|Product integration|
|Model optimization|APIs, UI, databases, AI workflows|

Think of it like this:

- **ML Engineer:** "How do I make this model accurate and scalable?"
- **AI Engineer:** "How do I build a product that uses this model?"
