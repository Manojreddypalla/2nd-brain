# Generative AI Engineer

> **A Generative AI Engineer builds applications powered by Large Language Models (LLMs) and other generative models.**

This is **not** the person training GPT-5 from scratch.

Most GenAI Engineers **use** foundation models to build products.

---

# Purpose

Take powerful foundation models like:

- GPT
- Llama
- Qwen
- Mistral
- Claude
- Gemini

and build products that solve real problems.

Examples:

- AI Tutor
- AI Coding Assistant
- AI Customer Support
- AI Document Search
- AI Medical Assistant
- AI Legal Assistant

# Difference from AI Engineer

This is subtle.

|AI Engineer|Generative AI Engineer|
|---|---|
|Works with many kinds of AI|Specializes in generative models|
|Computer Vision, ML, NLP, LLMs|Mostly LLMs and diffusion/multimodal models|
|Broad AI products|LLM-first products|
|General AI systems|RAG, Agents, Prompting, Tool Use|

You can think of it like:

```
AI Engineer
│
├── Computer Vision
├── Speech AI
├── NLP
├── Reinforcement Learning
└── Generative AI
        │
        ▼
Generative AI Engineer
```

So a GenAI Engineer is often a **specialized AI Engineer**.

# One important reality

A lot of people think this job is just:

> "Call the OpenAI API."

That's not what companies pay well for.

The valuable part is designing **reliable AI systems**, such as:

- Handling hallucinations.
- Retrieving the right documents.
- Building agent workflows.
- Managing context efficiently.
- Optimizing cost and latency.
- Evaluating AI output.
- Integrating AI with existing software.

That's what turns a demo into a production application.
