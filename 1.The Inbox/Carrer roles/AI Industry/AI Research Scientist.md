# AI Research Scientist

> **An AI Research Scientist invents new AI algorithms, models, and techniques to advance the field of Artificial Intelligence.**

**Category:** [[Artificial Intelligence]]
**Field:** [[Artificial Intelligence]]
**Related Roles:** [[Machine Learning Engineer]], [[AI Engineer]], [[Research Engineer]], [[Research Scientist]]

---

# Purpose

An AI Research Scientist pushes the boundaries of AI.

Instead of asking:

> "How can I use GPT?"

They ask:

> "How can we build something better than GPT?"

Their work creates the models and algorithms that engineers later turn into products.

---

# Why This Role Exists

Today's AI models didn't exist until researchers invented them.

Examples:

- Transformers
- Attention Mechanism
- Diffusion Models
- Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF)
- Mixture of Experts (MoE)

Research Scientists create these innovations.

---

# Responsibilities

- Read and analyze research papers
- Design new AI architectures
- Run experiments
- Train large models
- Evaluate new algorithms
- Publish research papers
- Collaborate with engineers
- Improve state-of-the-art performance

# Difference from AI Engineer

| AI Engineer | AI Research Scientist |
|--------------|-----------------------|
| Uses existing AI models | Creates new AI models |
| Builds products | Advances AI knowledge |
| Production focused | Research focused |
| APIs & Deployment | Papers & Experiments |

---

# Difference from ML Engineer

| ML Engineer | AI Research Scientist |
|--------------|-----------------------|
| Deploys models | Invents models |
| Optimizes production | Optimizes algorithms |
| Software Engineering | Scientific Research |
