- **What goes here:** This is the permanent database. This is where your subjects live, but keep the folders broad.
    
- **Examples of sub-folders here:**
    
    - `CSE & Software Arch` (Web Dev, DSA, OS, Node.js)
        
    - `AI & Agents` (LangGraph, Qdrant, RAG)
        
    - `Hardware & Mechatronics` (ESP32, Circuits, CAD, 3D Printing)
        
    - `Life & Media` (Gaming logs, Anime backlogs, Fitness)
        
- **Why it works:** You only need these 4 high-level folders. If you write a note about "Ohm's Law," drop it in `Hardware`. You rely on tags (`#electrical`) and internal links (`[[Resistance]]`) to organize the details inside that folder.
